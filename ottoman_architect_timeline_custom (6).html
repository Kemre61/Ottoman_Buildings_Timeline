Architect

This map presents the geographical spread of Ottoman architecture over time through the works of Ottoman architects, offering an interactive environment where users can trace both the rise, stagnation, and decline of the Ottoman Empire and the architectural journeys of its architects. By pressing the play button, users can automatically follow the flow of architectural production over time, or use the slider to move to any chosen date and explore the buildings of that period. In addition, by clicking on any architect in the legend, users can focus exclusively on the buildings associated with that particular architect.
